Your Privacy Matters

Bajeti Yanga stores your household financial data securely using Google Firebase, protected by industry-standard encryption.

We never sell your data to third parties. AI features process your spending summaries temporarily to generate responses — this data is not stored or shared.

Your data belongs to your household only, accessible via your household code and authenticated account.

Privacy Policy — Bajeti Yanga

Last updated: May 2026 · Rodart Creatives

1. Data We Collect

We collect your phone number or email for authentication, your household financial entries (income, expenses, budgets), and device information for app functionality. No names are required.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to provide the budgeting features of this app. Financial summaries are processed temporarily by Bajeti AI to generate budget insights — this data is not retained beyond the request.

3. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored in Google Firebase Firestore with enforced security rules. Only authenticated members of your household can read or write your data. Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers or third parties. Data is shared only within your household members who have your household code.

5. Your Rights

You may delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us. You can export your data as CSV from the Settings screen.

6. Contact

Questions? Email us at rodartcreatives@gmail.com or WhatsApp +265 888 029 029.
